Debbie's Veggie Lasagna

Gorgeous las from Debbie yet again.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • margarine
  • flour
  • salt
  • Debbie's magic
  1. Mix all the ingredients besides the magic.
  2. Preheat the oven to the temp of las.
  3. Cook.
  4. Remove from oven, and when no one is looking add the Debbie Magic.

Make this recipe ahead of time and store in refrigerator. Also, freezes well for a healthy instant dinner on a busy night!
